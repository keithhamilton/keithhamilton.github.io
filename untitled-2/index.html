Shoes

Two men, by all appearances completely unrelated, sit next to each other on the bus.

One man Hispanic, reading what some consider to be an opus of science-fiction, The Moon Is A Harsh Mistress.

The other man black, toting a Jumpman backpack that has seen casual wear for what appears to be a period of at least a year.

They have the exact same shoes on.
